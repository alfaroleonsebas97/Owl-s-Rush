Special Cards
X2 Card
Allows the player to play two turns in a row.
Rainbow Card
It goes for any color.
Rollback Card
Returns a token, of your choice, from another player to the space it was previously on.
Swap Card
It exchanges the position between the tile of the player's choice and the closest tile belonging to another player.
Allow landing on another player's space
If your owl will land on another player's owl, they exhange positions.
