HELP

The player who first takes all his tokens to the nest wins.

On the player's turn, he must choose one of his cards to move one of his owls.

When a player gets a Sun card, on his next turn he is forced to use it.
The player who completes the cycle of the sun must move back to the starting point his closest one to the goal.
The cycle of the sun restarts each time it completes.

There are some special cards that give advantages:
Card X2: card that allows the player to play two turns in a row.
Rainbow card: card that is valid for any color.
Rollback card: card that returns a token, of your choice, from another player to the field where it was previously.
Swap card: exchanges the position between the token of the player's choice and the closest token belonging to another player.
